About



Brunner Limited Company

Brunner Limited Company is a company registered in United Kingdom and Walles that offers general constructing services . The management of our company has over 30 years of experience in this field and is committed to perform the highest level of quality in construction services at fair and market competitive prices.

To maintain the highest level of professionalism Brunner Company Limited works only with qualified and authorized workers and is aligned with the United Kingdom building regulation and laws . We strive to offer the best working conditions for our workers to deliver extraordinary service and cost effective work execution.

Our company has finished projects in Greece, Italy and Romania in domestic construction and commercial spaces and offers a warranty certificate for work done in plumbing, heating and electricity that complies with United Kingdom standards and regulations.

We use only the best materials to execute our construction projects from United Kingdom or U.E and we offer promotional prices for the following executions.
